---
title: "Default Dashboard"
---

**Home** is the default dashboard automatically created by Mindlytics for each application. It provides a high-level snapshot of overall AI engagement using four preconfigured charts.

### Engagement Chart

Displays a 7-day overview of:

- Active users
- New users
- Total conversations
- Number of unique intents served by the AI

### Top Intents Chart

Shows the **top 10 most frequently used intents** over the last 30 days, based on intent count.

### Conversations Chart

Highlights conversational quality metrics for the last 30 days, including:

- Average conversation duration
- Average number of prompts per conversation
- Average number of intents served per conversation

### User Sentiment Chart

Displays a pie-chart distribution of **positive**, **negative**, and **neutral** sentiment detected in user interactions over the last 30 days.

The Home dashboard is read-only and serves as a quick, at-a-glance view of application health and engagement trends.
